About Me

writing letters to people

My favourite hobby is writing letters to people. I have a few pen-pals overseas and I find it very intersting to hear about their lives and what they enjoy. It doesn't take much effort but you get to know some wonderful people!

Horses

The horses I have met all live on farms. They are my favourtie animal as they are gentle yet extremely strong and powerful. Horses mainly eat grass or hay but are occasionally treated with carrots, bread etc. Horses are fiercely loyal.

Tam Jai

Tam Jai is a noodle dish originating from Sichuan, China. I usually order it with lots of veg and tofu on top. There are many branches of restaurant that you can go to but I usually go to the restaurant in Yat Tung as it is nearest my house.
